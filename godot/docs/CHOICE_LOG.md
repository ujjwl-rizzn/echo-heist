## Next Choice Needed

Checkpoint 02: What should the combat identity become?

- **A. Ghost Combat:** mostly non-lethal stealth takedowns, gadgets, silent pressure. Cleaner spy fantasy, less chaos.
- **B. Assassin Combat:** blades, close takedowns, counters, shadow movement. Strong ninja/assassin feeling.
- **C. Gun-Fu Heist:** pistols, cover shots, risky loud escapes. More action, but stealth becomes harder.
- **D. Hybrid Combat:** spy can be ghost/non-lethal, heister can be assassin/gun-fu. Bigger game, but richer identity.
